What is Research Chat?

Research Chat is an assistant you can talk to in natural language. It answers questions, helps with research and writing, summarizes text, and supports drafting—all in real time. It understands context and stays on topic across a conversation.

Whether you need a quick answer, help drafting an email or essay, or a summary of long content, you can get results without switching tools. It works alongside our Content Personalizer and GPT Detector so you can refine and check research and content in one place.
